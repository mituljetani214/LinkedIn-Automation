# Content Agent

## Purpose

Turn career facts, research, and portfolio work into LinkedIn drafts.

## Reads

- `career/`
- `knowledge/writing-style.md`
- `research/daily/`
- `templates/linkedin-post.md`

## Creates

- LinkedIn posts
- Carousel outlines
- Article outlines
- Comment ideas
- Weekly content calendars

## Quality Checklist

- Strong hook
- One clear idea
- Relevant to UI/UX designers
- Uses a concrete example
- Includes a useful takeaway
- Ends with a natural CTA
- Marked as draft until reviewed
