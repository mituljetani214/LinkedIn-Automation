# Writing Style

## Desired Style

- Clear
- Practical
- Reflective
- Specific
- Useful to UI/UX designers
- Confident without sounding inflated

## Avoid

- Generic motivational content
- Fake authority
- Overused hooks
- Unsupported claims
- Auto-posting without review

## Signature Themes

- SaaS UX
- Product design decisions
- Design systems
- Conversion improvement
- AI for designers
- Career growth
- Portfolio storytelling
