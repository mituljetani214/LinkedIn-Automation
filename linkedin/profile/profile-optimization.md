## About Section Draft

I am a Lead Product Designer and Lead UI/UX Engineer with 9+ years of experience designing SaaS platforms, enterprise applications, Shopify products, FinTech/accounting workflows, and AI-assisted product experiences.

My work sits between product design, UX strategy, design systems, and AI-powered workflow design. I help teams simplify complex business problems into intuitive user experiences that are easier to adopt, easier to scale, and easier to build.

At Identixweb, I lead UX and product design work across products such as Munim and iCart, collaborating closely with Product, Engineering, QA, Marketing, and Business stakeholders. I have led a team of 6 designers, established design quality standards, improved design-development collaboration, and contributed significant UX/product design work during Munim's growth from approximately 12,000 to 100,000+ active customers.

My design approach is human-centered and business-aware. Before designing screens, I work to understand the people, workflows, constraints, and product goals behind the problem. From there, I use research, journey mapping, information architecture, rapid prototyping, design systems, and AI-assisted workflows to move from ambiguity to useful product decisions.

I actively use AI across my design workflow, including UX research, product discovery, prompt engineering, documentation, design iteration, rapid prototyping, content generation, and workflow automation. I believe Human + AI is stronger than either alone, but every product decision must remain grounded in human needs.

Core strengths:

- Product strategy and UX strategy
- SaaS, enterprise, FinTech, accounting, and eCommerce UX
- Design systems and scalable UI architecture
- AI-assisted product design and prompt engineering
- UX research, information architecture, and journey mapping
- Cross-functional collaboration with Product and Engineering
- Team mentorship, design reviews, and design quality standards

I share practical lessons on AI-native product design, human-centered AI, design systems, UX leadership, and building products that solve real business problems.
